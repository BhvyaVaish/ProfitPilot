💡 Smart Tax-Saving Suggestions

Legally reduce what you owe — every rupee saved is profit.

🧾
Declare Business Expenses (खर्च दिखाएं)
Keep receipts for shop rent, electricity bills, and employee salaries. Each declared expense directly reduces your taxable income and lowers your Income Tax bill.
🔄
Claim GST Input Tax Credit (ITC)
Every time you buy goods from a GST-registered supplier, the GST you paid can be claimed back. This directly offsets your GST liability — potentially saving thousands per month.
💰
Section 80C Investments (निवेश करें)
Invest up to ₹1.5 Lakh annually in PPF, ELSS mutual funds, or NSC. This amount is directly subtracted from your taxable income before calculating Income Tax slabs.
📋
Presumptive Taxation Scheme (Section 44AD)
If your business turnover is under ₹2 Crore, you can declare 8% of turnover as profit under Section 44AD. This can significantly reduce the tax burden vs actual accounting.
