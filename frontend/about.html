Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of businesses is ProfitPilot built for?
ProfitPilot is designed specifically for Indian Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs). Whether you run a retail shop, a hardware store, an electronics outlet, or a grocery market, our platform adapts to your inventory style and billing needs without requiring an accounting background.
How will ProfitPilot help me manage my taxes?
We take the headache out of GST and Income Tax. As you generate bills, ProfitPilot automatically calculates category-wise GST. It also features a Tax Estimator that compares the New vs. Old Income Tax Regimes and checks if your turnover qualifies for Section 44AD or the GST Composition Scheme, helping you save money legally.
Do I need to manually enter my entire inventory one by one?
Not at all! If you already have a list of your products in Excel or another software, you can upload a simple CSV file to import your entire inventory in one click. You can also easily add new items manually whenever needed.
Is my business data secure?
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade cloud security and multi-tenant database architecture. This ensures your business data, inventory, and sales are strictly isolated and accessible only by you when you log in securely.
How is the AI Assistant different from a regular search bar?
Unlike generic chatbots, our AI Assistant is directly connected to your live business data. You can ask it questions in English or Hindi (like "What products should I restock before Diwali?" or "Mera sabse slow moving item kaunsa hai?") and it will give you specific, data-driven answers based on your actual sales velocity and local festival demand.
