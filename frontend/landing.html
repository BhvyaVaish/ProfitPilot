About ProfitPilot

Built for India's 63 Million MSMEs

India's small businesses are the backbone of the economy — yet most still run on notebooks, spreadsheets and guesswork. ProfitPilot was built to change that.

We combined inventory management, GST-compliant billing, real-time analytics, intelligent tax estimation and an AI assistant into a single free platform. It works in English and Hindi, runs on any device as a PWA and requires zero accounting knowledge to use.

Our mission is simple: give every shopkeeper the same data-driven intelligence that large retailers have — at zero cost.
